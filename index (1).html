Paradise


Tradition since 1876

The restaurant was opened in 1876, and in the late 19th century it became a key meeting place of the intellectual scene.

“At Paradise we create memories, without unnecessarily intimidating our Guests we encourage and engage in a discussion about interesting life and good taste. We want the “Paradise experience” to become an addictive experience.”

About

Paradise is the first restaurant of this type in Warsaw – classic and affordable. In Paradise you can try the most famous French warm dishes, such as ratatouille and Burgundy snails.

The Chef? Mr. Alberto himself

We are proud of our interiors.
